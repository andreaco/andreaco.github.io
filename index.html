FITNESS STRATEGY

The fitness function determines how fit an individual is (the ability of an individual to compete with other individuals). It gives a fitness score to each individual. The probability that an individual will be selected for reproduction is based on its fitness score.

CROSSOVER PROBABILITY

Probability of each element to be part of the Crossover phase. During this phase pairs of individuals are combinated in order to create a new Offspring characterized by the predominant features of the previous generation.

CROSSOVER STRATEGY

The combination of two patterns is made through an exchange of the values that compose them. The strategies apply one (Single Point) or two (Two Point) subdivisions of the two patterns and create the Offspring throung the exchange of the resulted sections.
